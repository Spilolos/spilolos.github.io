Our History

Brief History of Marvid

Marvid was founded in 2023 by Marce and David as a small boutique in Accra, Ghana. The brand quickly gained a reputation for its bespoke garments and attention to detail. Over time, Marvid expanded to include ready-to-wear collections and gained international recognition with its first runway show. The brand has since grown to include accessories, fragrances, and lifestyle products, all while embracing sustainability. Today, Marvid is known for its sophisticated and modern luxury, with a global presence and a dedicated following.
